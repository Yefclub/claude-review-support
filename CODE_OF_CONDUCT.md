# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1**. The full text is published at
<https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/> and is incorporated here by reference.

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this community a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity. We pledge to act and interact in ways that
contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our standards

Contributors are expected to behave respectfully and professionally: be considerate, welcome differing
viewpoints, give and accept constructive feedback gracefully, and focus on what is best for the community.
Behavior that the Contributor Covenant defines as unacceptable will not be tolerated. Refer to the linked
text for the complete list of expected and prohibited conduct.

## Scope

This Code applies within all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other project
channels) and when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Concerns may be reported privately to the maintainers via GitHub:

- Open a private report through the repository **Security** tab → **Report a vulnerability**, or
- Contact the maintainers via GitHub at [@Yefclub](https://github.com/Yefclub).

There is intentionally **no email contact**. All reports are reviewed and handled confidentially. Maintainers
will follow the Contributor Covenant's **Enforcement Guidelines** to determine consequences for any action
they deem in violation of this Code.
